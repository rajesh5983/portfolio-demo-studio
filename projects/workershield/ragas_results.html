Reranking the hybrid RRF candidates trades a little Context Recall for meaningfully better precision and faithfulness — the right tradeoff for a compliance assistant, where citing the right source matters more than retrieving every possible source.

What These Metrics Mean

Faithfulness

Does the answer stick to the retrieved context, or make things up?

Context Precision

How much of the retrieved context was actually relevant — less noise for the model.

Context Recall

Did retrieval find everything needed to fully answer the question?

Answer Relevancy

How directly the answer addresses the question that was actually asked.
